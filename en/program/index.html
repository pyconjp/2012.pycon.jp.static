Day3 : 2012/9/17 (Mon, National Holiday)¶

Doors open at 10:00 AM.

The PyConJP Development Sprints is the event where developers gather, and develop freely on whatever Python project they choose. You can participate in Sprint without having to participate in day 1 or Day 2 of the main PyConJP event. Participation is completely free, but lunch and tea are not provided.
